BHT Club Deposits & Withdrawals Methods and How to Use

How to Deposit & Withdraw on BHT Club - Complete Guide

Learn how to deposit funds and withdraw your earnings safely and quickly on BHT Club, Follow our step-by-step guide to ensure smooth transactions.

Deposit & Withdrawal Options on BHT Club

Our platform offers multiple deposit and withdrawal methods including UPI, Paytm, Bank Transfer, USDT (crypto), and AR Wallet for seamless and secure transactions.

Deposit Options

Method Steps Processing Time
UPI Open your UPI app → Enter the UPI ID that is shown on BHT Club Deposit request → Input amount → Confirm payment. Instant
Paytm / Wallets Open your Paytm app → Choose the recipient shown on BHT Club Deposit Account → Add amount → Complete transaction. Instant
Bank Transfer Use net banking → Transfer funds to the account shown on BHT Club → Reference your user ID. 15–30 mins
USDT (Crypto) Send USDT to our platform wallet address (TRC20/ERC20) → See step-by-step guide below. Instant – 10 mins
AR Wallet Use your AR Wallet in-app → Deposit funds directly → Earn bonuses from 2% to 5%. Instant

Withdrawal Options

Method Steps Processing Time
Bank Transfer Wallet → Withdraw → Enter amount → Choose your bank account → Confirm. 30 mins – 1 hour
Paytm / Wallets Wallet → Withdraw → Select Paytm → Enter amount → Confirm. Instant
USDT (Crypto) Wallet → Withdraw → Enter USDT address → Confirm → Use TRC20 network. Instant – 10 mins
AR Wallet Wallet → Withdraw → Select ARPay → Enter amount → Confirm. Instant

Step-by-Step: Adding USDT

  1. Open the Account section on your BHT Club Platform.
  2. Open the Withdrawal section on your account.
  3. Choose the USDT option.
  4. Click Add USDT if you haven’t added it yet. Follow the next step.
  5. Once you have entered the Customer service list, you can select the Add USDT and follow customer service instructions or contact our Telegram community for support.
  6. Once added, use the USDT address to withdraw funds using TRC20 network.
  7. You can also deposit using USDT (Rate: 94 INR per 1 USDT).

AR Wallet Overview

The AR Wallet allows members to deposit and withdraw faster with bonus rewards. Currently, bonuses range from 2% to 5% on all transactions.

Step-by-Step: Activating AR Wallet

  1. Register on BHT Club platform using the button below.
  2. Go to Account section from the homepage.
  3. Open AR Wallet.
  4. Register your AR Wallet account to start buying/selling ARB coin.

How to Buy ARB Coin (for Deposit)

  1. Open your AR Wallet account on BHT Club.
  2. Click on Buy Coin.
  3. Select the seller you want to buy ARB Coin from.
  4. Watch demo video → Confirm payment using UPI Ref ID → Close video.
  5. Use the UPI ID or QR code of the seller to pay.
  6. Copy UPI Ref ID or UTR number and screenshot the payment receipt.
  7. Go back to AR Wallet → Paste UTR number → Upload screenshot as proof.
  8. Verification completes within 1 minute → ARB coin is now usable.

Depositing Using AR Wallet

  1. Go to Account section.
  2. Click Deposit.
  3. Select AR Pay → Choose amount → Continue with payment.
  4. Deposit is added to your account instantly.

Selling ARB Coin on AR Wallet

  1. Open AR Wallet → Click Sell Coins.
  2. Click the “Sell ARB” coin icon.
  3. Select amount → Choose receiving method (e.g., UPI) → Add UPI if required.
  4. Set minimum limit (100% for full sale).
  5. Wait 1–30 mins for buyer payment → Confirm receipt once credited.
  6. Tick I have received the payment once confirmed.
  7. Receive 2% bonus for AR Wallet transactions.

Withdrawal to AR Wallet

  1. Account homepage → Click Withdraw.
  2. Choose ARPay → Enter amount → Click Withdraw.
  3. Enter login password → Confirm Withdrawal → Click Confirm to finalize.

AR Wallet Benefits

  • Faster deposits and withdrawals.
  • Earn bonuses: 2%–5% on deposits & withdrawals.
  • Invite friends → Earn 1.5% from their registrations.

Register Using an Invitation Link / Code

  1. Click the invitation link.
  2. Redirected to registration page.
  3. Fill in active phone number.
  4. Click Send to receive verification code.
  5. Enter verification code.
  6. Set secure password.
  7. Confirm password.
  8. Ensure invitation code field is filled.
  9. Agree to Privacy Agreement.
  10. Click Register.
  11. After registration, you can now access main AR Wallet page.

Become an AR Wallet Agent

  1. Register for an AR Wallet account.
  2. Go to MY → Team Center → Invite New Member.
  3. Share your unique link or code.
  4. Begin your role as an agent.
