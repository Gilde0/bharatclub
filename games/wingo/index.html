How to Play Wingo (Gameplay Mechanics)

Wingo is a game where you can predict colors, numbers, or sizes, each with different multipliers:

  • Size: Choose Small (0-4) or Big (5-9). Winning multiplies your bet by 2x.
  • Color: Red (0-2-4-6-8), Green (1-3-5-7-9), Violet (0 & 5).
    • Red or Green: normal hit → 2x, but if hit on 0 or 5 → 1.5x
    • Violet: 0 or 5 → 4.5x multiplier
  • Number: Predict the exact number to get 9x multiplier.

There is also a 2% service fee on every bet. For example, betting ₹100 will have a real bet amount of ₹98.
